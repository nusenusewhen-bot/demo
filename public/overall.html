FAQ

Answers to common questions

How does it work?+

Log in with Discord, choose a plan or start trial, then configure your automation settings in the dashboard.

Does it keep running if I close my browser?+

Yes. Campaigns and reply systems continue through cloud execution 24/7.

Can I use more than one account?+

Yes. v1 includes 1 account, v2 includes 3, and v3 includes 5 accounts.

What crypto do you accept?+

We accept Litecoin (LTC) only. A unique address is generated for each purchase.

What if I send the wrong amount?+

We accept up to $0.10 under or over the required amount. Anything else, contact support on Discord.

Is this affiliated with Discord?+

No. Veiled Adv is not affiliated with Discord Inc. Use at your own risk.
